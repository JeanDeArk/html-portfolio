Awesome Movies to Watch

3 Must Watch Movies

Spirited Away

It's a classic. Best of all, it's one Ghibli's best productions ever. The soundtracks are incredible and only add to the emotional journey that is Spirited Away.

The Day After Tomorrow

This movie is a masterpiece. It is beautifully executed, and tells a story about one of many natural disasters that may arise if our consumption of carbon fuels isn't addressed within one or two generations.

Ip Man

This movie is meant for martial arts and action movie fanatics. Depicting the story of the greatest martial artists to ever live, it shows how much the cast cared and respected the Grand Martial Arts Master Ip Man.
